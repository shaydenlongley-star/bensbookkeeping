Our Story

A Name You Can Trust
With Your Numbers

Ben's Bookkeeping Services was founded by Wendy Anderson — a single mom who built this business from the ground up to support her son and create something she could be proud of. What started as a personal mission became over 20 years of trusted service for individuals and small to medium businesses across Winnipeg.

We're home-based by design. Low overhead means we're not passing a fancy office lease on to you — just honest, accurate bookkeeping at a price that makes sense. From payroll and accounts payable to CRA correspondence and tax filing, we handle it all so you can focus on running your business.
